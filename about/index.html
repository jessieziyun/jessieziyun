Jessie Zhang is London-based designer and creative technologist.

Interested in people, nature and technology, her practice explores visual communication as a means to investigate and interrogate the world and ourselves. Her work spans multiple disciplines, from film and book design to photography and coding.

She has recently completed her BA (Hons) in Graphic Communication Design (with Creative Computing) at Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts London. Currently, she is a Future Producer at Somerset House. Her past experience includes internships at Georgia Hardinge and 原麦山丘. She was recently nominated for MullenLowe NOVA and Maison/0 Green Trail awards, and in 2020, she won a D&AD New Blood Awards Yellow Pencil.
