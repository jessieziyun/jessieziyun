Jessie Zhang is London-based designer.

Interested in people, nature and technology, her practice explores visual communication as a means to investigate and interrogate the world and ourselves. Her work spans multiple disciplines, from film and book design to photography and coding.

Currently, she is a Young Producer with Superflux at Somerset House and studying on the final year of her BA (Hons) in Graphic Communication Design (with Creative Computing) at Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts London.

Her past experience includes internships at Georgia Hardinge and 原麦山丘, and in 2020, she won a D&AD New Blood Awards Yellow Pencil.

Say hello at jessie.a.zhang@gmail.com or find her on Instagram and GitHub.
